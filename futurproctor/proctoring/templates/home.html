Capabilities

Advanced Proctoring Features

Comprehensive Monitoring for Exam Integrity

Face Verification

The system opens the exam only after facial verification. The student uploads their picture during registration, used across all exams to confirm identity.

Browser Tab Switching Detection

The system monitors tab switching and flags any unauthorized attempts during the exam in real time.

Object Detection

Detects unauthorized objects like books and smartphones during the exam using advanced computer vision.

Gaze Detection

Detects gaze movement to ensure candidates remain focused on the exam screen throughout the session.

Multiple Person Detection

Detects the presence of multiple individuals during the exam to ensure proper authentication and prevent impersonation.

Audio Analysis

Records and analyzes background sounds to detect any suspicious noises or conversations during the exam.

Secure Data Management:  All collected data is securely stored in PostgreSQL for comprehensive review and reporting.
