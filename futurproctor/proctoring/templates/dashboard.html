Important Rules for Online Exam

Please read all rules carefully before starting

1
Device & Internet: Ensure your device is fully charged or plugged in and has a stable high-speed internet connection for the entire duration of the exam.
2
Webcam & Microphone: Keep your webcam and microphone turned on at all times. These are mandatory for real-time monitoring during the exam.
3
No Unauthorized Tabs or Devices: Refrain from opening additional browser tabs, applications, or using external devices such as phones, smartwatches, or notes.
4
Stay in Frame: Ensure your face is clearly visible in the webcam frame throughout the exam. Avoid looking away from the screen frequently.
5
Quiet & Distraction-Free Environment: Select a quiet location where you won't be interrupted. Background noise or distractions may cause your session to be flagged.
6
Individual Effort Only: The examination is strictly individual. Any form of collaboration or communication with others is strictly prohibited.
7
Behavior Monitoring: AI systems and live proctors may monitor your activity including webcam and microphone feeds. Suspicious activities will be flagged for review.
8
Consequences of Violations: Any violation may lead to immediate termination of your exam session, invalidation of your results, and possible disciplinary action.
